What is anime?

Anime is a type of animated movie.
If you are a teenager, and want to watch a cartoon that isn't too "Little kid" fo you then anime is perfect!
There are animes for kids like Pokemon, Robo-Tech, and Super Book.
There are also animes for teens like Kaichu wa Maid Sama!, Fruits Basket, and Lull in
the Sea. Finally, there are 18+ animes like Gleipnir, Hagani, Sakurasou pet no Kanjo,
and Plunderer. Anime is also a genra, not a movie. Anime can be both a movie
or a series. I, personally, perfer the anime series more than the movie. But if you watch
anime just be prepared to see some really really really really really weird content.
